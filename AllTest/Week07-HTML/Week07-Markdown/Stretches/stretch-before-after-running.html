Strech before/ after running

Introducting streches before and after running. People who run short distance not only should focus on lower body streches but also on the whole body. Long distance running requires you to use not only your legs but your backbone and lower back. You should not try too hard but rather enjoy running.
1. Hold for 30 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

2. Hold for 30 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

3. Hold for 30 seconds. Slowly bend your knee and straight up
4. Hold for 20 seconds

5. Hold for 30 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

6. Hold for 30 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

7. Hold for 30 seconds

8. Up and down you knees for 30 seconds.

9. Hold for 30 seconds for each leg. Repeat 2 times
10. Hold for 20 seconds for each side. Repeat 2 times

11. Hold for 5 seconds. Repeat 3 times

12. Hold for 10 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg
13. Hold for 30 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

14. Hold for 30 seconds for each leg. Repeat 2 times

15. Hold for 30 seconds for each leg. Repeat 2 times
16. Circle the foot outward 10 times and repeat with the opposite foot

17. Hold for 20 seconds and repeat with the opposite leg

18. Hold for 5 seconds and repoeat 3 times
19. Hold for 20 seconds and repeat with the opposite arm

Daily care prevents injury or hurm by overusage. Don't look down on these and make it your habbit!
